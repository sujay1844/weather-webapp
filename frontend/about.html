A simple weather app

I built a simple weather app to get a deeper understanding of front-end development and APIs.
This project uses Openweathermap API.

I could have made it look prettier, added animations or more functionality. But you can't learn multiple things at once.

It has two backends: node and go.
I couldn't decide on a language for backend, so I tried out both NodeJS and Golang.

This app is deployed on Heroku here. It is free until November 28th, 2022. Uptime after that is uncertain.
